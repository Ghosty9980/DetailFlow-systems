We collect operational data (name, business email, revenue range) provided via our Audit request form. This data is used exclusively to evaluate your current business infrastructure and provide tailored automation recommendations.

We leverage industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your sensitive business data. Access to your information is restricted to essential personnel involved in the audit process.
